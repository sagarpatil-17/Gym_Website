Our Services

Cardio

Cardio helps lower your blood pressure by reducing the levels of bad cholesterol and raising levels of good cholesterol.

This is the zone where you burn the most fat and calories. Some of the most common examples of cardio include walking, cycling, and swimming.

Weight Lifting

Weight training provides a stress to the muscles that causes them to adapt and get stronger, similar to the way aerobic conditioning strengthens your heart.

Weight training is a common type of strength training for developing the strength and size of skeletal muscles.

Yoga

Yoga works on your internal organs and helps detox the body from within. This leads to a feeling of balance, inner fulfilment, and mental relaxation.

Yoga is essentially a spiritual discipline based on an extremely subtle science, which focuses on bringing harmony between mind and body.
